Salt and Pepper Calamari

Crispy, fresh-tasting fried calamari

A delicious appetiser ready in minutes.

Serve with your favorite aioli or sauce for dipping.

Ingredients List

  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 cups panko bread crumbs
  • 3 teaspoons cracked black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons sea salt
  • 1/2 kilogram calamari rings
  • 1/2 cup plain flour
  • vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Whisk egg and milk together in a bowl. In a separate shallow bowl mix the panko, pepper and salt.
  2. Lightly dust calamari with flour, shaking off any excess. Then dip into egg mixture followed by the panko bread crumb mixture to coat. Transfer to a plate and refrigerate for 20 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in a saucepan or deep fryer. Deep-fry calamari in batches until light golden brown, about 3 minutes. Drain on paper towels.
