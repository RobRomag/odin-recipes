Orange and Almond Cake

Orange Cake Using The Whole Orange "including the rind"

This Orange Cake has a nutty flavour, damp and sticky texture with a fantastic orange flavour.

Along with a unique method by which its made:

You blend whole oranges, rind and all with almond meal, sugar, eggs and baking powder.

No oil, no butter, no whisking required.

Ingredients List

  • 2 Whole fresh oranges
  • 2 3/4 Cups of Almond meal
  • 1 1/4 Cups of White Sugar
  • 6 Large Eggs
  • 1 1/4 tsp Baking powder

Steps

  1. Place oranges in a pot and cover with cold water. Bring to the boil over medium high heat.
    Boil for 10 minutes and drain.
  2. Repeat step one, two more times, put oranges back in the pot, cover with cold water and boil again for another 10 minutes drain then repeat one more time.
    Basically boil the oranges three times for ten minutes each time.
    Alternatively you can boil them for 40 minutes without draining just keep adding water as required.
    With the first option the cake flavour will be a bit cleaner because you're discarding the bitter water, however its effect on the flavour is minimal.
  3. Rinse oranges, cool them slightly so you can handle them. Chop them into smaller pieces ready for blending, remove any seeds then allow them to cool completely.
  4. Preheat oven to 160°C/320°F(140°C fan). Grease and line a 23cm cake pan with baking paper.
  5. Place chopped oranges in a food processor or blender. Blend on high until it's pureed into smooth consistency with only a few bits of rind remaining.
  6. Add almond meal, eggs, baking powder and sugar. Blend on high until all ingredients are combined approximately 5 to 10 seconds.
  7. Pour the mix into the prepared cake pan and bake for 60 minutes. When the surface is golden and you can inserted a toothpick into the centre and it comes out clean, its done!.
  8. Allow to cool and decorate as desired. Generally all thats required is a light dusting of icing sugar. "ENJOY!!"
